Sonic The Hedgehog 1991

Sonic the Hedgehog(1991) is a 2D platformer video game developed by Sonic Team and published by Sega for the Mega Drive/Genesis.The game was originally released in North America on 23 June 1991, followed by Europe in July and Japan on the 26th of the same month.Advertising its fast gameplay based on Sonic's extraordinary speed, the concept of a high-speed platform game was unique for its time and solidified the style of gameplay the series would be best known for.

Sonic X Shadow Generations
